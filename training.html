Welcome to the kazakh national board game Togyz Kumalak!
Let`s start your training!

Let`s start from the board.
Both players have 9 fields with 9 balls in each one!
Blue is opponents` side and red is your side

Also each player has bigfield for balls that were taken from opponent

Red border means it is your turn, so make a move

If the field have more than one arrow
One ball will remain in certain field
other balls will be laid on next fields counterclock-wise one by one
if the last field has even number balls after move
All balls on this fields will automatically go to your bigfield

When all balls will be laid on bigfields
Player who has more balls will be winner!
This is a end of the training! Good luck!
